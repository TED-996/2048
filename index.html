How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one! This version has mistake prevention mechanics which let you know if you're making a potentially dangerous move. The game assumes that the lower-right corner should have the best piece and, on the last line, the values should be decreasing right to left. To confirm a potentially dangerous move, hit Y. To abandon it, hit N or other directional keys.

How to select your playstyle: Click on the corner that you'd like to keep your best piece in. Click on one of the grey tiles on the edge to set the line on which the the values should be decreasing. If you try to set an invalid combination of line and corner (the corner doesn't belong to the line you picked), the background will turn red and you will not be able to save it. To save the settings, press the "Choose your playstyle button" again.
